Ban Appeal

Microsoft Community

To appeal your ban, you will need to meet the following requirements:

  • At least 120 days have passed since you were banned.
  • You understand and are able to explain why you were banned.

This is to make sure you've had the time to reflect on your actions, realise that there was justifiable reason for your ban and help you
acknowledge your mistake. These requirements are enforced for almost all appeals, though rare exceptions may be made for some special cases.

Note: if you were automatically banned for "matching patterns of known raiders", or banned
for reasons similar to "nitro scam, compromised account", you may appeal immediately
and disregard the requirements listed above.
